# openhealth-cypc-cancer-data
An automated n8n ETL pipeline extracting and cleaning Cancer in Young People in Canada (CYPC) data from the Government of Canada PHAC Health Infobase.


# Source
Source: Health Canada / Public Health Agency of Canada, Health Infobase API. Contains information licensed under the Open Government Licence – Canada. [https://health-infobase.canada.ca/api/]
